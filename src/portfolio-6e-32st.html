PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

Within the thriving NoMad submarket just north of Madison Square Park and surrounded by of-the-moment boutique hotels and restaurants, 30 West 26th Street is comprised of 12 floors totaling 97,900 square feet. The building is located between Broadway and Sixth Avenue. The office space is occupied by tech and service-related companies, and the ground floor is occupied by the renowned Hill Country Restaurant.
