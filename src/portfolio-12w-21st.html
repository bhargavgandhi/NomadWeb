PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

Located in the Flatiron District between Fifth and Sixth Avenues, 12 West 21st Street is a 12-story, 62,000-square-foot landmarked office building just a short walk from Madison Square Park, Gramercy Park and Chelsea. This stretch of 21st Street has been transformed by new residential and commercial developments which have been carefully preserved to retain the architectural integrity of this historic district. The building houses service sector and tech tenants and is anchored with a restaurant on the ground floor.
