401 PARK AVE S

PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

401 Park Avenue South is a 12-story office building comprised of 252,215 square feet of office and retail space. Located on the southeast corner of 28th Street the building is situated in one of the most desirable and burgeoning submarkets in New York. Adjacent to the 28th Street stop of the 6 Subway, 401 Park Avenue South is surrounded by boutique hotels, of-the-moment dining, nightlife and Madison Square Park. The building's high ceilings and an abundance of natural light are workplace characteristics that are highly attractive to technology, media, design and other creative users. The building features two newly renovated distinctive lobbies on Park Avenue South and 28th Street.
